快速插入时间戳

best way to insert timestamp in vim

  nmap <F3> a<C-R>=strftime("%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S %a")<CR><Esc>
  imap <F3> <C-R>=strftime("%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S %a")<CR>
